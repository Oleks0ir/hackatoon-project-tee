The Privacy Shield: TEEs

Your life story, locked in silicon.

🏦 The Virtual Vault

Your profile is instantly encrypted and sent directly into a Trusted Execution Environment (TEE)—a secure hardware enclave inside the cloud server's CPU.

🤝 Remote Attestation

Before data enters, your phone runs a mathematical check verifying that the vault is running only our verified, unmanipulated matchmaking code. Neither cloud providers, hackers, nor our own developers can read what's inside.
